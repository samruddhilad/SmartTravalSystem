🔬 The Science

How Bayesian AI Plans Your Trip

Our engine calculates conditional probabilities across mood, budget, weather, and group dynamics to deliver the mathematically optimal itinerary.

🎯

P(Destination | Mood, Budget)

We compute conditional probability P(dest | mood, budget, weather) using Bayesian CPTs to score every destination and pick the highest-probability match.

🗺️

Activity Inference

Activities are ranked by P(activity | destination, interest, weather), ensuring every hour of your day is both optimal and enjoyable.

💰

Budget Optimization

A probabilistic cost model distributes your budget across transport, accommodation, food, activities, and shopping for maximum satisfaction.

🔄

Plan A & Plan B

The engine automatically generates an alternative itinerary using the second-ranked Bayesian destination as a smart fallback.

💎

Hidden Gems

Scored by (1 - popularity) × uniqueness value, we surface off-beaten-path locations that perfectly fit your travel DNA.

🧳

Smart Packing

Packing lists are generated contextually from destination type, weather conditions, and the specific activities in your itinerary.
